What is Bookmark?

A bookmark is a saved link to a specific webpage, allowing you to quickly return to it later without needing to remember or search for the URL. Bookmarks are commonly used in web browsers to store frequently visited sites or articles you'd like to revisit. They save both the page URL and often its title or a custom label you provide.
